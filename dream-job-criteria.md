# Dream Job Criteria — Pranjal Mahna
# Used by /score and /hunt to evaluate role fit. Updated: May 2026.

---

## Identity Anchor
Senior BD/Partnerships executive. Builder-seller who creates the motion from 0-to-1.
Target titles: Director of BD, Director of Partnerships, Senior Manager BD, VP BD.
Target companies: AI-native, AI-platform, developer tools, edge computing, API-first.

---

## Scoring Dimensions (weighted, 0-10 each)

| Dimension | Weight | What "5" looks like | What "10" looks like |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ecosystem / partner motion | 25% | Manages existing partners, some co-sell, some enablement work | Build or scale an ISV/developer/channel ecosystem from scratch; co-sell, co-build, or API partner motion |
| 0-to-1 builder requirement | 25% | Some greenfield elements but established team and playbook exist | Founding or near-founding BD hire; greenfield territory; no playbook handed to you |
| Technical BD depth | 20% | Requires working knowledge of APIs or SaaS architecture | Requires fluency in APIs, SDKs, integration architecture, or AI/ML stack; or hands-on AI infra knowledge |
| AI-native / Edge AI angle | 20% | AI-adjacent company or AI feature within a broader product suite | Company is AI-first or the role directly touches AI infrastructure, NPU, SoC, GenAI, MCP/A2A |
| Seniority and scope | 10% | IC with clear ownership of a specific partner segment or vertical | Owns a P&L, partner count, or team; reports to VP or above |

**Score conversion:** multiply each dimension score by weight, sum to 0-100.
- 75-100 → Apply Now
- 65-74 → Apply with Bridge
- <65 → Pass (output score + one-line reason only)

---

## Hard Pass Filters (automatic Pass regardless of score)

- Requires proficiency in any language other than English
- Pure quota-carrying AE/AM role with no ecosystem or partner component
- Stated or estimated OTE below $150K
- Requires full-time on-site relocation outside Bay Area / NYC / LA / Seattle / Austin / Remote
- No AI, tech, or platform component in the company's core product
- Role reports below Director level internally (e.g., reports to a Senior Manager)

---

## Role Preferences (inform scoring, not hard cuts)

**Company stage:** Series B+ strongly preferred. Series A considered if founding BD hire with meaningful equity. Pre-seed/Seed = high risk flag (flag as WATCH OUT).

**Company type (ranked preference):**
1. AI-native infrastructure or platform (LLM providers, MLOps, edge AI, AI tooling)
2. Developer-first API companies (data, fintech infra, vertical SaaS with API-first motion)
3. VC-backed portfolio companies (True Ventures, a16z, Sequoia, GV portfolio)
4. Enterprise software with strong partnership/ecosystem layer

**Remote policy:** Remote-first or hybrid (1-2 days/week) strongly preferred. Full remote ideal.

**Reports to:** VP BD, VP Partnerships, Chief Revenue Officer, or CEO/COO directly. Not an individual contributor structure without clear ownership.

**Team ownership:** Must have either (a) a team to manage, (b) a partner count or revenue target to own, or (c) budget authority. Pure project/program BD roles are lower priority.

**Compensation floor:**
- Base: $180K+
- Total comp target: $250K-$350K+ (base + equity + bonus)
- Equity: meaningful for Series A-C stage; RSUs acceptable for public companies

## Strongest Alignment Signals (score modifiers)

When these signals are present in the JD or from research, add +0.5 to the most applicable dimension (cap any single dimension at 10). Note which signals fired in the role card output.

- "Build our partner ecosystem from scratch" or "first BD hire" → +0.5 to 0-to-1 builder dimension
- AI infrastructure, GenAI platform, edge computing, NPU/SoC → +0.5 to AI-native / Edge AI dimension
- ISV ecosystem, developer relations overlap, API monetization → +0.5 to Ecosystem / partner motion dimension
- MCP, A2A, agent-to-agent integration protocols → +0.5 to Technical BD depth AND AI-native / Edge AI dimensions
- Global scale expectation (APAC, EMEA, strategic accounts) → +0.5 to Seniority and scope dimension
- Founding team with operator background (not purely VC-driven) → +0.5 to 0-to-1 builder dimension

## WATCH OUT Triggers (flag these in every role card)

- Comp not disclosed (verify before advancing)
- Equity structure unclear (early stage with no stated range)
- Reports to Senior Manager rather than VP+
- Team <20 employees (execution risk, resource-thin)
- Role scope is pure AE with "BD" in title but quota-only structure
- Company hasn't shipped a production product yet
- No clear partnership/ecosystem precedent in their GTM
- High Glassdoor churn signal (rating below 3.5, frequent management reviews)
- Requires travel >50% without stated comp premium
- Non-English language requirement buried in JD (always check)
